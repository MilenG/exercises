Checklist Thesis Design

If you cannot answer a question with unambigous affirmation, write down which elements are precisely in need of improvement.

  1. Is all clear and neat? Do you have a proper evaluation?

  1. Is the research problem properly addressed?

  1. Does answering the research questionff indeed help in understanding the research problem or even in solving the research problem?

  1. Is the research problem grounded in the literature and has each section fo the thesis design relevant literature assigned to it?

  1. Is there a clear picture of the resources being used?

  1. Are the methods which are used described in enough detail, so that the supervisor can picture what will be done exactly?

  1. Is the evaluation appropriate in relation the research question?

  1. Is the risk assessment complete? Is it realistic? Is the back-up plan executable?

  1. Is the project plan complete and does it cover all sections?
